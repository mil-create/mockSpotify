Naked Tanya is a 4-piece Filipino band from Tuguegarao City, formerly known as Nude Tanya. Founded by Rolf, Domino, Angelo, and Zulu, the band evolved through lineup changes and now features Mark (vocals), Rowell (guitars), Zulu (bass), and Paul (drums). Their name reflects an "all-out" attitude, symbolizing raw vulnerability and passion. Naked Tanya delivers energetic music that serves as the "hearing aid for your dying ears."
