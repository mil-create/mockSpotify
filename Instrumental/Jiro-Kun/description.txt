Jiro-Kun is the math rock and instrumental solo project of Jayro Cuntapay, a musician from Tuguegarao City, Philippines, and the lead guitarist/vocalist of Ang Huling Pahina. Inspired by CHON, Plini, and Gabba Santiago, Jayro crafts intricate, emotional compositions blending technical precision and storytelling. Through Jiro-Kun, he explores dynamic song structures and complex rhythms, reflecting his lifelong passion for instrumental music.
