///ice is a singer-songwriter and producer from Tuguegarao City. Inspired by MYX at age 10 and his family's musical tastes, he began writing songs in 5th grade. Starting with a ukulele at 13 and later mastering guitar and piano during the lockdown at 15, ///ice shared his music via Instagram stories. By 2023, after a pivotal moment watching an old video, he embraced opportunities as an independent artist. Guided by a love for cinema, he crafts narrative-rich compositions, blending personal experiences with storytelling.
