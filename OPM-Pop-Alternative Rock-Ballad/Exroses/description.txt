Exroses is a Tuguegarao-based band blending energetic and heavy sounds into a unique harmony. Aaron "The Moon" Escobar lays dynamic basslines, Angelo "The Time" Reyes powers the beats on drums, and Karlpaolo "The Sun" Articulo crafts precise rhythmic riffs. James Louis "The Snow" Articulo weaves melancholic guitar leads, while James Paul "The Halo" Salinda's rugged yet angelic vocals tie it all together. Representing celestial elements, Exroses creates music that balances power, emotion, and precision.
